How to play the game?

Survive, avoid the enemy aircraft and shoot them down

What to do in the Cockpit?

Avoid the still enemies on your sides by pressing Left, Right

Speed up the plane and slow down with Up, Down

Shoot down the ones in center by pressing 'Enter'

Enemies can shoot at you, be wary of laser and glass cracking

Pause the game with 'Escape'
